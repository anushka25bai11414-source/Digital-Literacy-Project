## 📄 SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS:

--------------------------------------------------------
## Do's
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Keep your Profile picture and Bio professional, especially on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn.
2. Think before you post, and keep the information as accurate as possible.
3. Use privacy settings alertly, to protect your confidential details.
4. Be gracious while commenting or participating in discussions online.
5. Post your projects and achievements in such a way, that builds your reputation.


--------------------------------------------------------
## Don'ts
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Never share your personal and sensitive details like Phone number, OTPS, Passwords or Bank details online.
2. Refrain from posting insensitive or offensive content that can cause harm to your image.
3. Do not indulge in Trolling, Cyberbullying or Online fights.
4. Ignore clicking on Suspicious links or replying to unknown/spam messages.
5. Avoid posting anything that could potentially affect your academic or career opportunities negatively.
